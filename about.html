I also love Tex-Mex (especially queso), dogs, and beautiful sunsets. At school, in College Station, I spend my free time painting, exploring the town with my friends, and building.

While abroad, I had the opportunity to explore many cities throughout Italy, Spain, and France. From this fantastic experience, I gained many Auburn friends from all different places, and I will always cherish the incredible memories we made together from our exciting adventures abroad.
